Our Clinic

We are an extension of the St. Joseph’s Health Centre Urology Department, dedicated to providing expedited assessment and specialized care for urological concerns. Our clinic focuses on rapid diagnostic pathways to minimize wait times and ensure timely access to expert urological care.

Our team of experienced urologists brings together expertise in prostate health, kidney conditions, urinary tract issues, and urological oncology. We are committed to providing patient-centred care with a focus on clear communication, evidence-based treatment, and streamlined pathways to diagnosis and treatment.

Our Urologists

Our clinic features experienced urologists from St. Joseph’s Health Centre, each bringing specialized expertise to provide comprehensive urological care.
